              Project Description

Plantiva

Plantiva is an AI-based plant disease analysis system specially build to help farmers.users need to upload the pictures of the diseased plants or crops , the AI would analyze and suggest the possible ways to cure it and proper medications required.



CopBot



CopBot is an AI-powered ChatBot which is used to clarify public’s doubts about Indian Law and Order rights and acts.



Book Emulator



Book Emulator is AI-driven tool for students where their staffs could upload notes and study materials and students can access the notes via AI and summarizes their answers.



DeepFake Detector



DeepFake Detector is a tool which is used to detect images or videos or texts which were morphed or generated using AI.



Hear Connect



Hear Connect is tool which is especially built for deaf people where they can convert their sign language into text.



Voice Assistant



Voice Assistant is a AI agent which perform tasks just by simple commands it’s built along with the system , it also enables blind people to use computer.
